The **AI & Machine Learning Mastery** course is designed to prepare you for interviews at top tech companies like Google, OpenAI, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft, and Anthropic. This comprehensive course covers everything from ML fundamentals to advanced LLM concepts, production ML systems, and interview preparation.

### This course blends:
- **Detailed Article-based explanations** for conceptual clarity
- **AI-generated study notes** for revision and quick learning
- **AI-powered quizzes** (Easy, Medium, Hard) to test your knowledge
- **Hand-picked resources** (papers, videos, books, tools) to deepen your expertise

By the end, you'll have the **skills, mindset, and confidence** to excel in AI/ML interviews and build production-ready AI applications.

## What You'll Learn

This course is divided into carefully crafted modules covering both **theory and practice**:

1. **AI Fundamentals and Core Concepts** - ML basics, algorithms, evaluation, preprocessing
2. **Deep Learning Fundamentals** - Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, Transformers
3. **Large Language Models and Generative AI** - LLMs, GPT, fine-tuning, RAG, deployment
4. **Natural Language Processing** - NLP tasks, embeddings, classification, QA systems
5. **ML System Design and Production** - MLOps, deployment, monitoring, scalability
6. **AI Interview Preparation** - Interview questions, coding challenges, system design
