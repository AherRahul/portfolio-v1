When dealing with databases in Java, one of the key pieces in the puzzle is how we retrieve and handle data. This is where the **ResultSet** comes into play. Picture it as a table of data that you can iterate through, allowing you to access and manipulate the rows returned from your SQL queries.

It's not just a data container; it offers methods to navigate through the data and extract information meaningfully.
