The `Statement` interface in JDBC is a fundamental piece of the puzzle when it comes to executing SQL queries against a database. While it may seem simple at first glance, understanding its full capabilities and limitations can significantly impact how you interact with your data.

The `Statement` interface allows you to execute SQL statements directly, and its features can come in handy when you need basic operations without the overhead of prepared statements or callable statements.
