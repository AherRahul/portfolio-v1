The ability to connect to resources over the network is pivotal for many applications today. If you've just wrapped your head around the `URL` class, you're in a great position to dive deeper into the next layer of networking in Java—`URLConnection`.

This class is a key player when it comes to working with connections to URLs, enabling you to read from and write to the resources identified by those URLs.
