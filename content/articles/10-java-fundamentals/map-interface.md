The `Map` interface in Java is a fundamental part of the Collections Framework, acting as a bridge between keys and values. It’s like a dictionary, where each key maps to a specific value. Understanding how to efficiently use `Map` can significantly enhance your ability to manage and retrieve data in your applications.

Let’s delve deep into the `Map` interface, exploring its structure, methods, and various implementations, alongside practical examples and use cases that highlight its power and versatility.
