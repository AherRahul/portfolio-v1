In the world of data structures, choosing the right one can make or break the efficiency of your application. If you’ve worked with `LinkedHashMap` and enjoyed its predictable iteration order, you’re in for a treat with `TreeMap`.

This data structure not only maintains insertion order but also keeps its keys in a sorted order, which opens up a whole new realm of possibilities for efficient data management.
