# Join the Community

Learning system design gets easier (and more fun) when you’re not doing it alone.

AlgoMaster has an active community across a few places, and you can join in wherever you prefer. Here are the best ways to participate:

*   **Join the discussion on each lesson**: Every topic has a comment section at the bottom. Ask questions, share your takeaways, and reply to others. If a comment helped you, leave a like so more people find it.
*   [**Join our Discord**](https://discord.gg/rCtXvbFx9Z): This is the best place for real-time discussions, help, and peer learning. Introduce yourself, share what you’re working on, and jump into ongoing conversations.
*   [**Subscribe to the newsletter**](https://blog.algomaster.io/): Get weekly system design content: insights, explanations, patterns, and updates from the course.
*   **Share feedback:** We are working hard to improve the content every day. If something feels unclear, missing, or could be better, your feedback helps us make AlgoMaster the best place to learn software engineering and prepare for interviews.
