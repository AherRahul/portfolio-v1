# Data Compression

Data compression is a crucial technique in modern computing. Whether you are managing databases, optimizing web applications, or handling large-scale media streaming, compression helps reduce storage costs, improve performance, and minimize network bandwidth usage.

For example, consider a **video streaming platform** like Netflix. Without efficient compression, streaming a high-resolution video would consume excessive bandwidth, leading to buffering issues and increased infrastructure costs. Similarly, large-scale **log storage systems** need compression to keep costs manageable while maintaining quick access to historical data.

*   Why data compression is important
*   Lossless vs. lossy compression

*   Popular compression algorithms
