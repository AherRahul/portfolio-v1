## Design Principles Quiz

Answer the questions first, then check the key.

## Questions
1. What does DRY stand for?
2. What does KISS encourage?
3. What does YAGNI mean?
4. Why avoid tight coupling?
5. What is Separation of Concerns?
6. What is the Law of Demeter?

## Answer key
1. Don't Repeat Yourself.
2. Keep solutions simple.
3. You Aren't Gonna Need It.
4. It makes change and testing harder.
5. Keep responsibilities focused and independent.
6. Only talk to immediate friends.
