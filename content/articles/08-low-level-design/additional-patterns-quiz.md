## Additional Patterns Quiz

Answer the questions first, then check the key.

## Questions
1. What does Repository pattern abstract?
2. What is MVC?
3. Why use Dependency Injection?
4. What does Producer-Consumer solve?
5. What does Thread Pool manage?

## Answer key
1. Data access.
2. Model-View-Controller separation.
3. To reduce coupling and improve testability.
4. Coordinating work with queues.
5. Reusable worker threads.
