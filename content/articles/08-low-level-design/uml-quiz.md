## UML Quiz

Answer the questions first, then check the key.

## Questions
1. What does a class diagram show?
2. What does a sequence diagram focus on?
3. When do you use a use-case diagram?
4. What does an activity diagram represent?
5. Why use UML?

## Answer key
1. Classes and their relationships.
2. Message flow over time.
3. To show actors and system goals.
4. Workflow and control flow.
5. To communicate design visually.
