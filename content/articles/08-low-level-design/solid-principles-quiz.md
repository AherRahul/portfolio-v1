## Solid Principles Quiz

Answer the questions first, then check the key.

## Questions
1. What does SRP state?
2. What does OCP encourage?
3. What does LSP ensure?
4. What does ISP recommend?
5. What does DIP promote?

## Answer key
1. A class should have one reason to change.
2. Open for extension, closed for modification.
3. Subtypes can replace base types safely.
4. Prefer small, specific interfaces.
5. Depend on abstractions, not concretions.
