# Time when you missed a deadline

Question

##### Tell me about a time when you missed a deadline. What did you learn?

In the world of software development and product creation, despite our best efforts with agile planning, roadmaps, and sprint commitments, one thing is certain: at some point, something will go wrong. Scope will be underestimated. An unexpected bug will appear. A key team member will get sick.

Deadlines will be missed.

This is not a sign of failure; it is a reality of working on complex problems. What defines a professional is not their ability to avoid this reality, but how they react when it happens. Do they hide the problem? Do they blame others? Or do they face it head-on with proactivity and accountability?
